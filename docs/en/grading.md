# Grading Criteria

| **Week** | **Deliverable** | **Max** | **Score tiers** |
|---|---:|---:|---|
| **W0** | Preparation check | 0 | Completion only. No submission required. |
| **W1** | Standing demonstration | 20 | **20** — upright \(\geq\) 15 s, full system in frame<br>**12** — achieved but \(<\) 15 s or marginal<br>**0** — not achieved |
| **W1** | Safety & assembly visibility | 5 | **5** — full assembly visible<br>**0** — not visible |
| **W1** | Video clarity | 5 | **5** — all key parts unobscured throughout<br>**3** — key behavior still identifiable<br>**0** — unusable |
| **W2** | Position control | 8 | **8** — inversion \(\geq\) 10 s within \(\pm\) 0.5 m of set-point<br>**5** — inversion held but position drift large<br>**0** — inversion lost within 5 s |
| **W2** | Encoder | 7 | **7** — A/B waveforms labeled *and* direction reversal verified<br>**5** — waveforms shown, direction not verified<br>**2** — pulse count only, no phase analysis<br>**0** — not submitted |
| **W2** | Simulation | 5 | **5** — \(x(t)\), \(\dot{x}(t)\) plots sim vs hardware *and* discrepancy explained<br>**3** — plots included, no explanation<br>**1** — qualitative description only |
| **W3** | Enhanced model | 6 | **6** — wheel inertia + friction + numerical params<br>**4** — two of three elements<br>**2** — one of three elements<br>**0** — no enhancement over W2 |
| **W3** | Load test | 7 | **7** — stable \(\geq\) 5 s *and* load mass/location identified<br>**5** — stable but load unquantified<br>**2** — attempted with documentation<br>**0** — not submitted |
| **W3** | Controller comparison | 7 | **7** — PID metrics (rise time, overshoot, settling) + structurally different controller (LQR / state feedback / feedforward) with same metrics<br>**4** — PID only, or same-family alternative<br>**2** — qualitative discussion only |
| **W4** | Slides | 10 | **10** — all four elements with data: theory, design, validation, lessons<br>**7** — three of four elements<br>**3** — \(\leq\) 2 elements or text-only |
| **W4** | Demo video | 10 | **10** — stable \(\geq\) 10 s *and* enable/disable sequence clear<br>**6** — stable but enable/disable sequence unclear<br>**2** — demo fails but honestly documented<br>**0** — not submitted |
| **W4** | Q&A | 10 | **10** — \(\geq\) 2 technical questions answered correctly<br>**5** — answers broadly consistent with submitted materials<br>**0** — not answered / inconsistent |

*Partial credit may be applied within each sub-criterion.*
